How to Carve a Pumpkin

Step 1: Gather Your Materials

Before you start carving your pumpkin, make sure you have the following materials:

  • A pumpkin
  • Sharp carving tools
  • Newspaper or a disposable tablecloth
  • A marker for drawing your design
  • Tea light or LED candle

Step 2: Prepare Your Workspace

Spread newspaper or a disposable tablecloth on your work surface to catch pumpkin guts and prevent a mess.

Step 3: Cut Off the Top

Using a sharp knife or pumpkin carving tool, carefully cut off the top of the pumpkin to create a lid. Angle the cut slightly so that the lid doesn't fall inside.

Step 4: Scoop Out the Insides

Use a scoop or your hands to remove the seeds and stringy bits from the pumpkin's interior. You can save the seeds for roasting!

Step 5: Design Your Pumpkin Face

With a marker, draw your desired face or design on the front of the pumpkin.

Step 6: Carve the Design

Following your drawn design, carefully carve out the pumpkin using your carving tools. Take your time and work slowly to avoid mistakes.

Step 7: Place a Light Inside

Insert a tea light or LED candle into the pumpkin and place the lid back on top. Light the candle to illuminate your pumpkin!
