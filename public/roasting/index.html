How to Roast a Duck

This is the description of the steps to roast a duck.

  1. Gather Your Ingredients
  2. Preheat Oven
  3. Seasoning
  4. Apply Melted Butter
  5. Apply Rest of Butter
  6. When-Finished

Gather Your Ingredients

2 teaspoons paprika
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 (5 pound) whole duck
1/2 cup butter, melted, divided

Preheat Oven

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

Seasoning

Combine paprika, salt, and pepper in a small bowl; rub spice mixture onto skin of duck. Place duck in a roasting pan.

Apply Melted Butter

Spoon 1/4 cup melted butter over duck and continue cooking for 45 minutes more.

Apply Rest of Butter

Spoon remaining 1/4 cup melted butter over duck and cook until golden brown, about 15 minutes more.
